Community Guidelines

Our shared commitment to creating a safe, respectful, and supportive space

Welcome to our climate change community. This platform exists to provide a safe, calm, and inclusive space for people around the world—especially those affected by climate change—to share experiences, emotions, and knowledge.

These guidelines help ensure that everyone feels heard, supported, and respected. By participating in this community, you agree to follow these guidelines and help create a positive environment for all.

Core Principles

Safety First

This is a safe space. We prioritize the emotional and psychological safety of all members. Harassment, bullying, or any form of abuse will not be tolerated.

Empathy & Respect

Everyone's experiences and emotions are valid. Show empathy, respect different perspectives, and remember that we're all navigating climate change together.

Inclusivity

We welcome people from all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives. Discrimination of any kind is not allowed.

Supportive Communication

Use language that is supportive, kind, and constructive. We're here to lift each other up, not tear each other down.

Do's

  • Share authentically: Your experiences and emotions are valuable. Share them honestly and respectfully.
  • Listen actively: Take time to read and understand others' perspectives before responding.
  • Offer support: When appropriate, offer words of encouragement and support to others.
  • Verify information: When sharing facts or data, cite reliable sources and verify information.
  • Report concerns: If you see content that violates guidelines, report it using our reporting system.
  • Respect privacy: Don't share personal information about yourself or others without consent.

Don'ts

  • No harassment: Do not harass, bully, threaten, or intimidate other members.
  • No hate speech: Discriminatory language, hate speech, or attacks based on identity are prohibited.
  • No misinformation: Do not spread false information or unverified claims about climate change.
  • No spam: Do not post spam, advertisements, or irrelevant content.
  • No trolling: Do not post inflammatory or disruptive content intended to provoke others.
  • No personal attacks: Criticize ideas, not people. Personal attacks are not allowed.

Reporting System

We have a content reporting system to help maintain a safe community. If you encounter content that violates our guidelines, you can report it using the report button on posts or comments.

What to Report:
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Spam or misinformation
  • Inappropriate content
  • Any other violation of community guidelines

Our Commitment: All reports are reviewed by moderators. We take reports seriously and will take appropriate action to maintain a safe community. False reporting may result in consequences.

Moderation

Our community is moderated to ensure these guidelines are followed. Moderators may:

  • Remove content that violates guidelines
  • Warn users about guideline violations
  • Temporarily or permanently restrict access for repeated violations
  • Take other actions necessary to maintain community safety

Moderation decisions are made with the goal of maintaining a safe, supportive environment for all members. If you have questions about moderation actions, you can contact us through appropriate channels.

Emotional Safety & Mental Health

Climate change can be emotionally challenging. This community is designed to be a supportive space, but it's important to remember:

  • Your feelings are valid: It's normal to feel anxious, sad, angry, or overwhelmed about climate change.
  • Take breaks: If discussions become too overwhelming, it's okay to step away and take care of yourself.
  • Seek professional help: If you're experiencing severe emotional distress, please seek help from mental health professionals.
  • Support others: Be mindful of others' emotional well-being when sharing content that might be triggering.
  • Use trigger warnings: When sharing potentially distressing content, consider using content warnings.

Remember: This platform is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline in your area.

Thank You

By participating in this community, you're helping create a safe, supportive space for people affected by climate change. Thank you for your commitment to these guidelines and to each other.
