Learn About Climate Change

Verified resources from trusted scientific and health organizations

Understanding climate change is essential for addressing its impacts. Below are verified resources from leading scientific and health organizations that provide accurate, evidence-based information about climate change, its causes, impacts, and solutions.

Note: We prioritize verified sources to ensure you receive accurate information. Always be critical of information sources and verify claims with multiple trusted sources.

Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC)

The IPCC is the United Nations body for assessing the science related to climate change. It provides policymakers with regular scientific assessments on climate change, its implications, and potential future risks, as well as options for adaptation and mitigation.

What You'll Find:
  • Comprehensive climate science reports
  • Assessment of climate change impacts
  • Mitigation and adaptation strategies
  • Regional climate projections
  • Special reports on specific topics
Visit IPCC Website

NASA Climate Change

NASA's Climate Change website provides comprehensive information about climate change based on decades of Earth observation data. It offers accessible explanations of complex climate science, visualizations, and the latest research findings.

What You'll Find:
  • Climate data and visualizations
  • Evidence of climate change
  • Climate science explained simply
  • Interactive tools and resources
  • Latest climate news and research
Visit NASA Climate Website

WHO Climate Change and Health

The World Health Organization provides information on how climate change affects human health. This resource is particularly important for understanding the health impacts of climate change and how to protect yourself and your community.

What You'll Find:
  • Health impacts of climate change
  • Climate-related health risks
  • Public health adaptation strategies
  • Mental health and climate change
  • Resources for health professionals
Visit WHO Climate & Health
